Predictive Analytics for Economics Students

For Grade 12 Economics students, predictive analytics in economic forecasting helps predict future trends in the economy using past data and patterns.
Key Concepts in Simple Terms
1. Time Series Forecasting – Looks at past economic data (e.g., inflation, GDP) to predict future trends.
2. Regression Analysis – Studies how one economic factor affects another (e.g., how interest rates impact spending).
3. Machine Learning – Uses computer programs to analyze large amounts of data and make predictions.
4. Sentiment Analysis – Examines news and social media to understand how people feel about the economy.
5. Scenario Forecasting – Predicts economic outcomes based on different situations, like changes in oil prices or government policies.

MeasureMate App for Amateur Seamstresses

A handy tool for tracking, converting, and organizing sewing measurements.
Key Features:
1. Measurement Tracker – Store and organize body and fabric measurements.
2. Unit Conversion – Convert between inches and centimeters.
3. Pattern Size Guide – Standard and customizable size charts.
4. Project Planner – Track materials, notes, and progress.
5. Seam Allowance Calculator – Adjust for seam allowances.
6. Fabric Estimator – Calculate fabric needs based on garment type.
7. Visual Guides – Sewing tutorials and measurement illustrations.
8. Sync & Backup – Save and export data across devices.
